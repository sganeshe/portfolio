About Me

I'm Saumya, a cover artist crafting bold, story-driven visuals for the Indian hip-hop scene. I've worked with artists like Panther and Prabh Deep, creating cover art that captures the raw energy and narratives of their music.

My design style blends gritty textures, expressive typography, and cultural elements, aiming to translate sound into striking visuals that stand out on streaming platforms and social media.
